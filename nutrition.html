Dog Nutrition

Learn how to keep your dog healthy with balanced diets and feeding tips.

Balanced Diet

Dogs thrive on a diet that balances proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals. Proteins build muscle, fats provide energy, and carbs support digestion. Vitamins and minerals keep bones strong and immune systems healthy.

Feeding Guidelines

Puppies need 3–4 meals a day, adults usually 2, and seniors may need smaller, more frequent meals. Portion sizes depend on breed size, age, and activity level. Always follow vet recommendations.

Special Considerations

Working dogs, pregnant dogs, and those with medical conditions may require tailored diets. Grain-free, raw, or prescription diets should only be chosen with veterinary guidance.

Hydration

Clean, fresh water should always be available. Dogs can dehydrate quickly, especially in hot weather or after exercise.

Supplements

Omega-3 fatty acids, glucosamine, and probiotics can support joint health, skin, and digestion — but only under vet advice.
