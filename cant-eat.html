Foods Dogs Should Avoid

Some foods are dangerous for dogs. Keep these off their plate.

Dangerous Foods

  • Chocolate: Contains theobromine, toxic to dogs.
  • Grapes & Raisins: Can cause kidney failure.
  • Onions & Garlic: Damage red blood cells.
  • Avocado: Contains persin, harmful to dogs.
  • Alcohol & Caffeine: Dangerous to the nervous system.
  • Xylitol: Artificial sweetener that causes hypoglycemia.

Why They’re Harmful

These foods can cause vomiting, diarrhea, seizures, organ damage, or even death. Dogs metabolize substances differently than humans, so what’s safe for us can be deadly for them.

Safe Alternatives

Instead of harmful foods, offer safe treats like carrots, apples (without seeds), blueberries, or specially formulated dog biscuits.

Emergency Response

If your dog eats something toxic, contact your vet immediately. Quick action can save lives.
